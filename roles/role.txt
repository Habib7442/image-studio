## üéØ Team Size Recommendations by Scale

### **Startup (0-10K Users)**
- 1 Full Stack Developer (wearing multiple hats)
- 1 DevOps/Security Engineer (part-time or consultant)

### **Small Team (10K-100K Users)**
- 1 Solution Architect/Tech Lead
- 2-3 Full Stack Developers
- 1 DevOps Engineer
- 1 QA Engineer (part-time)

### **Medium Team (100K-1M Users)**
- 1 Solution Architect
- 2 Frontend Specialists
- 2 Backend Developers
- 1 DevOps Engineer
- 1 Security Engineer
- 1 QA/Test Automation Engineer
- 1 DBA (part-time or shared)

### **Large Team (1M+ Users)**
- 1-2 Solution Architects
- 3-4 Frontend Developers
- 3-4 Backend Developers
- 2 DevOps Engineers
- 1 Security Engineer
- 1 SRE
- 1-2 QA Engineers
- 1 DBA
- 1 Data Engineer
- 1 Platform Engineer

### **Enterprise (10M+ Users)**
- All roles above with multiple specialists
- Dedicated teams for each domain
- 24/7 support and on-call rotations
- Specialized compliance and governance roles
